To cite Tesseroids in publications, please use:     

Uieda, L., E. P. Bomfim, C. Braitenberg, and E. Molina (2011), Optimal forward 
calculation method of the Marussi tensor due to a geologic structure at GOCE 
height, Proceedings of the 4th International GOCE User Workshop. 

A PDF version of this article is available at
http://fatiando.org/papers/Uieda,etal._2011.pdf

A BibTeX entry for LaTeX users is

@inproceedings{,
    author = {Uieda, Leonardo and Bomfim, Everton P. and Braitenberg, Carla and 
              Molina, Eder},
    title = {Optimal forward calculation method of the Marussi tensor due to a 
             geologic structure at GOCE height},
    booktitle = {Proceedings of the 4th International GOCE User Workshop},
    year = {2011},
    url = {http://fatiando.org/papers/Uieda,etal._2011.pdf}
}
